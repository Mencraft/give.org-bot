Giving Coach AI

Giving CoachAI is an interactive digital window into the research and thinking of Give.org.

Giving CoachAI is designed to answer questions about philanthropy and charitable giving, especially when and how to trust charities when giving. It contains information from across Give.org, including podcasts, videos, blogs, and reports.

Try asking questions like these:
"How can I avoid getting defrauded with my donation?""How can I safely donate my car?"
